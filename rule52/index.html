FAQ

Questions buyers will actually ask.

Is this just a template bundle?

No. The public site routes buyers through Stripe into a gated, server-side generator. The generator uses intake fields to produce organization-specific documents and control files. Templates are not the product. The generated pack is.

Is it AI-powered?

Rule 52 is an AI policy generator, not an AI chatbot. The v1 generator uses structured server-side logic to create policy/control packs. That keeps the output consistent, defensible, and cheaper to run.

Does this replace legal or compliance review?

No. These materials are for general business use. Regulated, high-risk, or contract-bound organizations should have qualified legal, privacy, cybersecurity, clinical, tax, or compliance professionals review before adoption.

What happens after payment?

Stripe redirects buyers to the generator access page. The system verifies payment, creates a tier-specific entitlement, and lets the buyer complete a guided intake. If they close the tab, the recovery page can restore access from the purchase email once payment records exist.

Can consultants use this for clients?

Only with the Consultant or Agency license. Starter and Pro are internal-use tiers for the purchasing organization. Consultant and Agency tiers add client-use rights but still prohibit resale, redistribution, or listing raw files as a competing template product.
