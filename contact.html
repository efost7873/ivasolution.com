Useful starting points.

A specific workflow. A delayed decision. A report that keeps getting rebuilt. A handoff that repeatedly fails. A role carrying responsibility without enough authority. A process everyone knows is wasteful but nobody has clean authority to change.

What to include.

Keep the first message simple. IVA does not need a polished internal memo before the conversation can start.

The issue

Describe the workflow, approval chain, reporting burden, handoff, decision, or operating problem you want to examine.

The cost

Share what it is costing now: time, staff capacity, rework, missed deadlines, delayed decisions, unclear ownership, or repeated correction.

The goal

Explain what needs to change. Faster decisions, less duplicate work, clearer ownership, better reporting, cleaner handoffs, or more usable operating visibility.
