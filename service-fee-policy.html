Service and Fee Policy

Clear service terms before the work starts.

This policy summarizes IVA pricing, minimums, travel terms, rush rates, payment expectations, and proposal requirements for five-ledger advisory, diagnostic, implementation, workshop, and support services.

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Standard rate.

IVA’s standard hourly rate is $200/hour unless a written proposal, statement of work, invoice, or approved rush arrangement states otherwise.

Hourly advisory and session minimums.

Hourly sessions are used when the scope is best handled as advisory, preparation, facilitation, or focused working time rather than a fixed-fee project.

$200/hour

Fixed-fee work requires a written proposal or statement of work. Starting prices and standard ranges guide scope, but complex work is quoted by proposal.

Travel policy summary.

No travel premium applies for in-person work within 75 road miles one way of IVA’s Illinois business base.

For more than 75 and up to 150 road miles one way, mileage may be billed at the current IRS standard business mileage rate. Travel time beyond the first 75 road miles each way may be billed at 50% of the standard hourly rate. A 3-hour on-site minimum applies unless otherwise agreed.

For more than 150 road miles one way, air or rail travel, or overnight travel, a written travel estimate or written approval is required before travel is booked. Travel time may be billed at 50% of the standard hourly rate unless otherwise agreed. Mileage, airfare, rail, rental car, rideshare, parking, tolls, lodging, meals, and incidentals are reimbursable. A 4-hour on-site minimum applies unless otherwise agreed.

Rush, payment, and proposal terms.

Rush, after-hours, weekend, or holiday work is billed at $250/hour if approved in writing before work begins.

Payment terms

Virtual advisory calls are prepaid before scheduling. Other hourly sessions are confirmed by invoice unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Written scope

Fixed-fee work requires a written proposal or statement of work before work begins.

Invoices and confirmation

In-person, group, executive, board, diagnostic, implementation, and support engagements are confirmed by written proposal or Stripe invoice.
